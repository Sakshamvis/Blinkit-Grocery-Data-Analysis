# Blinkit-Grocery-Data-Analysis
## Overview
- Built an interactive Power BI dashboard to analyze Blinkit grocery sales data
- Focused on understanding sales trends, product performance, and outlet behavior
- Designed the dashboard to support quick, data-driven business decisions/
--- 
## Business Questions Covered
- How does fat content impact total sales?
- Which item categories perform best?
- How do sales vary across outlet size, type, and location?
- Does outlet establishment year affect performance?
---
## Key KPIs
- Total Sales
- Average Sales
- Number of Items Sold
- Average Rating
---
## Dashboard Visuals
The report includes the following visualizations:

- **Total Sales by Fat Content**  
  - Donut Chart  
  - Shows sales contribution of low-fat vs regular products  

- **Total Sales by Item Type**  
  - Bar Chart  
  - Highlights top- and low-performing product categories  

- **Fat Content by Outlet for Total Sales**  
  - Stacked Column Chart  
  - Compares outlet performance segmented by fat content  

- **Total Sales by Outlet Establishment Year**  
  - Line Chart  
  - Analyzes sales trends based on outlet age  

- **Sales by Outlet Size**  
  - Donut Chart  
  - Evaluates the relationship between outlet size and sales  

- **Sales by Outlet Location**  
  - Map Visualization  
  - Shows geographic distribution of sales  

- **All Metrics by Outlet Type**  
  - Matrix  
  - Displays all KPIs in a single consolidated view

## Key Insights
- Certain item categories contribute significantly to total sales
- Medium and large outlets generate higher revenue compared to smaller outlets
- Sales performance varies notably across outlet locations
- Older outlets show more stable sales trends over time
